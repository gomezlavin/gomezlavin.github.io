Projects

ALTIA HEALTH (Aug 2014) (Co-Founder)

Altia Health aspires to organize all healthcare data in Latin America and make it accessible and useful for physicians and patients. The app enables physicians to send accurate and understandable prescriptions directly to patients and pharmacies from the point of care.


Technologies: Ruby on rails, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS3.

ME-2-ME (Dec 2013) (Individual Project)

Me-2-Me is an app designed to help you keep track of your thoughts, goals and epiphanies by reminding you about them unexpectedly in the future. You can also write letters to your future self that will be only available on a specific date, as if they were placed inside a time capsule. Use Me-2-Me and start the dialogue with yourself!

Technologies: Ruby on rails, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS3.

   www.me-2-me.com

   github.com/gomezlavin/me2me

OOMERANG (Nov 2013) (Group Project)

Oomerang is a geolocation-based lost & found app to help find and return lost things to their owners. By using specific locations on a map oomerang simplifies the process of connecting people that loose and find items. Lost it, found it, oomerang it!

Technologies: Ruby on rails, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS3, Google Maps API, Google Places API.

   www.oomerang.it

   github.com/gomezlavin/oomerang

About Me

Hi, I am Santiago Gomez Lavin, a software engineer who is passionate about technology and coding. I am versed in Ruby, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Rails, Node.js, Express, Jade and Stylus, although my favorite language is definitely Javascript.

Despite graduating with a Computer Engineering degree, I spent the first years of my professional career working in finance and renewable energy while living abroad in Europe, Southeast Asia and Latin America. It was only after taking a year off from work when I realized that my true passion had always been technology and software, so I made my way to San Francisco to start working as a full-stack web developer, and I love it!

I believe that technology is the key to create a more organized, efficient and egalitarian world, where everyone can enjoy life in abundance. My life's goal is to help make this a reality by creating great software. When I am not coding you'll probably find me travelling, enjoying the outdoors, reading, dancing or running.
